Bangladesh Flood Relief (2022)

This project will help thousands of people affected by the worst flood in Bangladesh with food relief, clean water, hygiene products
medical support, and financial assistance.

This project will also enable volunteers and JAAGO to help with evacuation and relief centres and outreach services, and support victims and communities to recover and build their lives after the flood.
